Path Pal: Your Personal College Planning Companion

Navigate your path to college success with Path Pal, the all-in-one mobile app that puts personalized college planning at your fingertips. Whether you're just starting high school or preparing your applications, Path Pal guides you every step of the way.

KEY FEATURES

AI College Counselor
Get instant, personalized advice 24/7 from your virtual college admissions counselor. Ask questions about applications, course selection, test prep, and more. Your AI counselor is always available to help you make informed decisions about your college journey.

Admissions Odds Calculator
See your real-time acceptance probability for any college. Get AI-powered tips on how to improve your chances of getting in. Understand where you stand and what you need to work on to reach your dream schools.

What If Simulator
Test different scenarios before you make changes. What if you raised your SAT score? What if you added more activities? See how hypothetical changes affect your admission odds so you can plan strategically.

College Explorer
Discover thousands of colleges with powerful search and filtering tools. Find schools that match your budget, location preferences, test requirements, and more. Search by name, filter by acceptance rate, tuition, test requirements, and student population.

Career and Salary Insights
Explore career paths and median salaries by major. Make informed decisions about your future before you commit to a major. Understand the real-world outcomes of your academic choices.

Personalized Activity Recommendations
Get tailored suggestions for extracurricular activities and internships that strengthen your college applications. Build a well-rounded profile that stands out to admissions officers.

Four-Year Planner
Build your complete roadmap from freshman year to graduation. Track courses, activities, test dates, and important milestones. Stay organized and on track throughout your high school journey.

Saved Colleges
Bookmark your favorite schools and track your application progress all in one place. Keep everything organized as you research and apply to colleges.

Complete Profile Management
Keep all your academic information, test scores, and goals organized in one secure profile. Your data stays private and is used to provide personalized recommendations just for you.
